About SEO Melbourne

Founded in 2009, SEO Melbourne was born from a simple vision: to help local businesses thrive in the digital landscape. Our founder, Mark Peterson, recognized that most small and medium-sized businesses were struggling to compete online despite having exceptional products and services. What started as a two-person operation has grown into a powerhouse team of over 50 SEO specialists, content creators, and digital strategists. Over the past 15 years, we've helped more than 500 businesses across Melbourne and beyond achieve their digital marketing goals, generating millions in additional revenue for our clients. Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, adaptation to algorithm changes, and an unwavering commitment to delivering results that matter.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to democratize access to world-class SEO services and empower businesses of all sizes to achieve online success. We believe that every business deserves the opportunity to be found by its ideal customers. At our core, we're driven by transparency, integrity, and a genuine passion for our clients' success. We don't believe in quick fixes or black-hat tactics—instead, we build sustainable, long-term strategies that create lasting value. Our core values include transparency in all our communications, accountability for every campaign, continuous innovation to stay ahead of industry trends, and a client-first approach where your success is our success. We're not just an SEO agency; we're strategic partners invested in your growth and committed to delivering measurable, meaningful results that transform your business.
